About AskDwell

AI Property Agent — Company Introduction

Who We Are

AskDwell is an AI-powered property management assistant built for the modern rental industry. We help property managers and landlords automate the time-consuming work of tenant communication, maintenance coordination, leasing operations, and daily property tasks — 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

Founded by a team with deep roots in real estate operations and AI development, AskDwell was born from a simple observation: property managers spend the majority of their time on repetitive, low-complexity tasks that pull them away from growing their portfolios. We built AskDwell to change that.

What We Do

AskDwell acts as a tireless AI property agent for your properties — handling everything from tenant inquiries and lease term lookups to maintenance scheduling, rent payment status checks, and even lifestyle services like food delivery recommendations, professional laundry, and local restaurant and hospital searches.

Our platform integrates seamlessly with industry-standard tools including Buildium, Google Workspace, WhatsApp, Discord, and SMS — so your team keeps working the way they already do, just with an intelligent assistant doing the heavy lifting.

Core capabilities include:

  • Repair and maintenance request handling — intake, routing, vendor coordination, and tenant updates
  • Repair work scheduling — automated scheduling with vendors, confirmations, and follow-ups
  • Lease term search and tenant Q&A — instant, accurate answers from your actual lease documents
  • Rent payment handling and status checks — tenants can pay, check balances, and get receipts automatically
  • Lifestyle concierge services — food ordering, professional laundry, restaurant and hospital search, and more
  • 24/7 tenant support — across WhatsApp, email, SMS, and Discord

Our Mission

We believe every property — whether a single-family home or a 500-unit portfolio — deserves the kind of attentive, responsive management that used to require a full operations team. AskDwell makes that level of service accessible, affordable, and automatic.

Why Now

The property management industry is at an inflection point. Tenant expectations are rising — they want instant responses, digital-first interactions, and the kind of service they get from consumer apps. At the same time, property managers are stretched thin, managing more units with fewer resources.

AskDwell bridges that gap. We're not replacing property managers — we're giving them a force multiplier that handles the volume so they can focus on the work that actually requires human judgment.
